Some perk ideas the user can pick:

PEOPLE:
worksome population: the population works harder (multiplying the food production etc.)
happy people: The base mood of the people is a little higher
compulsory education (Schulpflicht): The education expenses are cheaper (but can't be set to 0)

MILITARY:
cheap military: Reduces yearly costs of military units
loyal servants: Reduces chances of military units to desert in battle

PLAYER:
luck: Fewer bad events at the beginning of the turn
